Cookie Policy

This cookies policy applies to the Europeana website https://metis-sandbox.europeana.eu.

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you through cookies or similar technology.

This Policy describes why and how we use cookies on our websites europeana.eu and to pro.europeana.eu, the types of cookies we rely on, the specific cookies we use, and their duration and function.

When navigating our websites, you can change your cookies settings using the cookie banner or your browser settings.

Why do we use cookies?

Europeana uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our websites, including keeping you signed in, remembering your navigation preferences, understanding how you use our website and facilitating a safe navigation.

What types of cookies do we use?

This site uses functionality cookies, analytics cookies and feedback cookies. The sections below list the cookies we use by type, clarifying their purpose, their provider, their duration and the website in which we use them.
